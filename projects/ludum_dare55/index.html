Ludum Dare 55

During the latter half of my second semester in college, I joined and participated in the world’s largest and longest-running “Game-Jam” - Ludum Dare. In this online competition, thousands of enthusiastic developers rush to complete a game within a limited time bracket of up to 48 hours (Compo) and 72 hours (Jam).

I participated in the Compo for a shortened period over the weekend, but never the less landed 277th place overall of 1582 ranked entries
